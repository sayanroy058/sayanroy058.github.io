Govt. Sponsored Multipurpose School (G.S.M.S.) for Boys,Taki House

The admission process for Govt.Sponsored Multipurpose School(Boys)Taki House starts every year in the month of November and School starts in January.For admissions to Govt.Sponsored Multipurpose School(Boys)Taki House, parents should do following steps:

  • Schedule appointment with admissions office of the School.
  • Get registration form either from School office or from School website.
  • Fill up registration form and submit with necessary documents to admission office.
Eligibility:
  • For Nursery admissions, the age limit is at least 2 years and 6 months as on May 31st.
  • For class I, the age limit is 5 years and 6 months as on May 31st.
Required Documents:
  • Completed registration form.
  • Self attested Copy of birth certificate.
  • Transfer certificate, mark sheet of the last exam.
  • Passport size photograph.
  • Please visit the School for more information of admission.
